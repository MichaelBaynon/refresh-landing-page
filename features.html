5 Features Every Lambda School Student Needs to Know

1. Custom Missions

Upon account completion, you'll receive the power to choose your own adventure. More specifically: you will be guided through a short survey -- no text inputs required! -- that will help you isolate the missions or quests that will have the greatest impact in your life. Along the way, you'll encounter dungeons and boss battles you must conquer to "level up" your potential.

2. Built In Motivation

You're not alone in the fight to improve your health and fitness. No man or woman is an island, so Refresh borrows some tricks from social media apps. Daily, you'll be exposed to a feed full of Lambda School students who are making strides in their quest to build healthy habits. You can also join a team of peers led by your TL, SL, or an instructor who will encourage you to refresh your system on a daily basis.

3. Points Based Approach

This app is intentionally designed to feel like a video game. You'll earn points every time you check off a healthy habit. Stack two or three related habits together to double or triple your total score. If you land on top of the Refresh Leaderboard, you could win an Amazon giftcard or some sweet Lambda swag. We also provide cool reports in the form of charts and graphs that vividly illustrate how much you've acheived.

4. Social Media Integration

No need to tell us your email address. Simply integrate your account with Facebook, Twitter, or GitHub. You'll receive instant access to a dashboard where you can customize your path to a healthier, happier life. And unlike all the other stuff you downloaded for Lambda School, we won't constantly clog up your inbox with spam.

5. Cool Animated Celebrations

Every step you take towards a healthy lifestyle should be celebrated. As you complete quests and missions, we'll celebrate with a colorful animation that's guaranteed to make you smile. Team-based achievements get the best animations, because we want to emphasize the effectiveness of a community-based approach.
