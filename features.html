5 Features Every Lambda Student Needs to Know

Refresh is built with you, the average Lambda School student, in mind. Below are the top five benefits of downloading our healthy habit app.

1. Custom Missions

Upon account completion, you'll receive the power to choose your own adventure.

More specifically: you'll be guided through a short survey -- no text inputs required! -- to help you pick a quest that's a good fit for you.

Along the way, you'll encounter dungeons and boss battles you must conquer to "level up" your potential.

2. Built In Motivation

You're not alone in the fight to improve your health and fitness.

No man or woman is an island, so Refresh borrows some tricks from social media apps.

Daily, you'll be exposed to a feed full of Lambda School students who are making strides in their journey to build healthy habits.

You can also join a team of peers led by your TL, SL, or an instructor who will encourage you to refresh your system every single day.

3. Points Based Approach

This app is intentionally designed to feel like a video game.

You'll earn points every time you check off a healthy habit.

Stack two or three related habits together to double or triple your total score.

If you land on top of the Refresh Leaderboard, you could win an Amazon giftcard or some sweet Lambda swag.

We also provide nifty reports in the form of charts and graphs that vividly illustrate how much you've accomplished.

4. Social Media Integration

No need to tell us your email address.

Simply integrate your account with Facebook, Twitter, or GitHub.

You'll receive instant access to a dashboard where you can customize your path to a healthier, happier life.

And unlike all the other stuff you downloaded for Lambda School, we won't constantly spam your inbox (you're welcome!).

5. Cool Animated Celebrations

Every step you take towards a healthy lifestyle should be celebrated.

As you complete quests and missions, we'll celebrate with a colorful animation that's guaranteed to make you smile.

Team-based achievements get the coolest animations, because we want to emphasize the effectiveness of using a support community.
